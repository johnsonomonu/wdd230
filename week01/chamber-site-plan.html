Site Purpose

The Carlsbad Chamber of Commerce acts as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener, and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.

Another purpose could be to provide a platform for local businesses to connect, collaborate, and grow.
